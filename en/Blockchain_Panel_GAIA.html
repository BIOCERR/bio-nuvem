What you are seeing

A didactic model of BIOBlock: chained blocks by previous hash, mempool with pending transactions, validators/miners, and a short fork resolved by longest chain rule.

  • Integrity: any alteration breaks the hash and is rejected.
  • Transparency: real-time batch audit.
  • Automation: smart contracts enforce rules between Matrix and MCFFPs.
  • Traceability: from produced IBC to local bottling and logistics.
